How It Works?

Simply sign up for an account, then you can join rooms to share your questions, and engage with a community of peers who are ready to help. Users can participate in discussions, share their knowledge, and collectively work towards understanding and solving academic challenges.

Step 1

First step is to create your account and login, then you will have
an access to join a room to solve your question.

Step 2

After that you need to wait others to join you, you can join others as well.

Step 3

Rooms designed to have 3 people. Room owner will share their
question on interactive whiteboards and as a group you will solve the questions.
